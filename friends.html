Eric's friends

The characters, that mostly hang out with Cartman are the other main characters of South Park - Stan, Kyle and Kenny.

Kyle Broflovski

Eric Cartman and Kyle have a complicated relationship. They're frenemies, always getting into arguments and pranks. But deep down, they care about each other in their own weird way.

Stan Marsh

Cartman and Stan are best friends in South Park. They have their ups and downs, but they always stick together through thick and thin. Their friendship is a big part of the show!

Kenny McCormick

Cartman and Kenny have an interesting dynamic. Cartman often makes fun of Kenny and his unfortunate fate of dying in almost every episode. But despite that, they still hang out and have some funny moments together.
